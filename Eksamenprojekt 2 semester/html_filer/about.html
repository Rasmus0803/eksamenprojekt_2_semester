FAQ'S

What is KeyControl?

KeyControl is a system which companies can access. KeyControl helps companies to get a clear visuel overview of subscriptions and a safe database with the companies passwords.

How does KeyControl work?

The company inserts all data about subscriptions and passwords into KeyControls database. The KeyControl will create a structure that makes it easy and simple for companies to get an overview over which subscriptions that are active and when they expire.
